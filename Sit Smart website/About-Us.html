Our Story & Mission

Great to meet you all !!! My name is Nic and I joined the AIoT Coding and Engineering Skills Education for Gifted Students' programme held by City University of Hong Kong and this is my project.

Idea

I get the idea because I am suffered from backpain due to incorrect sitting posture, especially when I need to study from home last year. 
I then realize the importance of a correct sitting posture, especially to the development of young children. Actually, sitting incorrectly for a long time will cause a lot of problems, including back & neck pain, Chronic pain & poor balance,  Varicose veins, etc. Therefore, maintaining a good sitting posture is really important.
Because of that, I want to build an app or web application that can help monitor our sitting posture, and remind us if we are sitting incorrect.FeaturesAs my initial target users is young children, I include some games to make the application more attractive as well as encourage the young children to sit straight in order to get the reward (playing the gameðŸ˜‚)
But after making the application, I found out it can benefit all people, from young children to students to adults. Therefore, I add some useful health information about sitting (see resources) and posture analysis (coming soon!)
